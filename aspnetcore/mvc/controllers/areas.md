<span data-ttu-id="c851b-142">MVC は区分でのビューのレンダリングを試みる場合、既定では、次の場所で検索を試みます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c851b-142">When MVC tries to render a view in an Area, by default, it tries to look in the following locations:</span></span>
